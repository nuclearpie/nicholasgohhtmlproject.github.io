Dine Sports Recreation

Basketball Court

If you prefer a fun group activity, book the basketball or multi-purpose courts located right next to the Bungalow Reception. Basketball is provided with the rental of the basketball court. The multi-purpose outdoor court is available for street soccer or badminton.

Practical Information

For Bungalow users only. To book the court, simply drop by the Bungalow Reception and check with our friendly Customer Service Officers on its availability.
