Dine Sports Recreation

Bowling Alley

There are 20 championship bowling lanes available for leisure and professional bowlers to practise. The kids can also give this sport a try with our eight bumper lanes. If you’re looking to engage in some competitive bowling, you’re welcome to join the Club’s bowling team which participate in various bowling leagues and competitions throughout the year.

Besides bowling, the Resort Bowl also offers facilities such as karaoke rooms, board games and a games arcade!
